The 16-Week Initiation

This rite of passage unfolds in four phases:


Month One — Safety
You learn how to feel safe in your body and nervous system


Month Two — Sight
You learn how to see clearly without distortion, avoidance, or illusion


Month Three — Truth
You learn how to live, speak, and move in alignment with who you truly are


Month Four — Wholeness
You integrate so that you no longer fragment under pressure


This is not self-improvement
